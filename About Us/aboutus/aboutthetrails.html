About Pacific Trails Resort

Our Story

Pacific Trails Resort was founded with a vision to create a peaceful coastal retreat where guests can reconnect with nature. Located along the scenic California coastline, our resort offers breathtaking ocean views and peaceful surroundings where you can sit back and enjoy nature.

What began as a small collection of yurts of a small family, has grown into a destination known for relaxation and outdoor adventures. We are committed to preserving the beauty of the land and its' natural habitants, while providing a memorable experience for every guest who visits us.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a tranquil escape that gives you the opportunity to rest, explore, and connect. Whether you are hiking, enjoying the ocean breeze, or relaxing by the fire. We at Pacific Trails Resort offers an experience that renews both body and mind.
